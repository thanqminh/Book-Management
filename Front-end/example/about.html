About

Managing your way.

The traditional book manage process can take many months of effort and more money than most sortters. It’s no wonder authors get discouraged.

You shouldn’t have to ask anyone’s permission to manage your own book! We have removed the hurdles inherent in traditional book management by combining amazing advances in technology with a sophisticated all-in-one manage solution for both print books and eBooks.

While powerful technology makes it possible for anyone to manage a high-quality book, our collaboration capabilities make BookManagement the easiest, fastest, and most exciting way to manage.

At any point in the manage process you can share knowledge, gather feedback from reviewers and editors, and collaborate with other authors, all without leaving BookManagement.

BookManagement is Software in the Cloud – so you don’t have to download anything to your computer. It’s the fastest and easiest way to manage print books and eBooks—anywhere!
